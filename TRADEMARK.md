# Consulting-as-Code™ — Trademark & Usage

Consulting-as-Code™ (CaC™) is a trademarked term owned by
VuduVations.

This document defines its proper usage and boundaries.

---

## What Consulting-as-Code™ Refers To

Consulting-as-Code™ refers specifically to:

- A deterministic, multi-agent reasoning system
- That replaces the consulting pyramid
- By computing strategy through enforceable logic
- With validated math and constraint enforcement

---

## What Does NOT Qualify

The following are **not** Consulting-as-Code™:

- Chatbots
- AI copilots
- Transcript summarizers
- Slide generators
- Single-model LLM systems
- “AI-powered consulting” services

Using LLMs alone does not constitute Consulting-as-Code™.

---

## Proper Usage

Correct:
- “Consulting-as-Code™ computes strategy deterministically.”
- “Consulting-as-Code™ replaces labor with logic.”

Incorrect:
- “Consulting-as-Code™ is an AI assistant.”
- “Consulting-as-Code™ helps consultants work faster.”

---

## Enforcement

Use of the term to describe systems that do not meet
the architectural and logical criteria outlined in this repository
constitutes misrepresentation.

This repository serves as the **definitive reference**.
