# Parallel Reasoning

Consulting-as-Code™ does not work faster by working harder.

It works faster by working **in parallel**.

This is the single most decisive advantage over human consulting.

---

## Why Human Teams Are Sequential

Human consulting teams are constrained by:
- Meetings
- Hand-offs
- Calendar availability
- Cognitive load
- Knowledge loss between roles

Even the best teams follow this pattern:

Interview → Synthesize → Review → Revise → Present → Rework

Each step waits for the previous one to finish.
Latency compounds.

---

## Why Agents Are Parallel by Design

Agents:
- Operate simultaneously
- Share structured memory
- Challenge outputs instantly
- Do not wait for approval
- Do not fatigue

In Consulting-as-Code™:
- Pain extraction
- Budget analysis
- Constraint detection
- Stakeholder mapping
- Financial validation
- Roadmap sequencing

…all occur **at the same time**.

There are no meetings.
There are no hand-offs.
There is no knowledge decay.
